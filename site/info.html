Widget Guide

Room & Board

A glanceable dashboard for your personal screen: your commute, your weather, your markets, your images.

Every widget is added and configured right on the board, or from your phone. 32 widgets, six groups.

Display Modes

Dashboard

The board always shows your widget grid.

Screensaver

The board always shows full-screen ambient scenes (see Screensavers below).

Scheduled

The dashboard shows during the daily windows you set; the screensaver takes over the rest of the time.

Making it yours

Two buttons sit in the bottom-right corner of the board. Everything you can change lives behind one of them. Rather configure things from your computer? Visit roomboard.app/setup to pick and customize your widgets, then enter the pairing code shown on the board when you’re done.

Edit layout

Rearrange the board itself: drag cards to move them, resize them, remove one, or add a new widget from the picker. The board shows each card’s minimum size while you work, so nothing ends up too small to read. Tap Done when you’re finished.

Settings

Everything else: your display mode and schedule, screensaver sources, which stations, teams, tickers, and news outlets you follow, °F or °C, 12- or 24-hour time, and your display name.
