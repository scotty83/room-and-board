Add photos to your board

Set up an iCloud shared album or GDrive folder (either one, or both), check the link here, and get a short code to type on your board.

For your board to read iCloud or Google Drive photos, the album or folder must be shared with a public link. It's unlikely anyone would randomly discover it, but anyone with the link could view these photos — if you're not comfortable with that, don't add personal photos. And if your board is displayed in a publicly visible space, it's your responsibility to make sure the photos are appropriate for it.

iCloud Shared Album

  1. Open the Photos app on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap Collections, then Shared Albums
  3. Tap + to create an album, name it (e.g. Office Board Photos), and add at least one photo. Add more anytime and the board picks them up automatically
  4. Open the album, tap the button, then the button
  5. Turn on the Public Website slider
  6. Tap Copy Link and paste it below

Google Drive Folder

  1. In Google Drive, choose + New, then New folder, and name it (e.g. Office Board Photos)
  2. Upload at least one photo. Add more anytime and the board picks them up automatically
  3. Tap the folder name, then Share
  4. Under General access, switch to Anyone with the link
  5. Tap Copy link (it looks like drive.google.com/drive/folders/…) and paste it below
