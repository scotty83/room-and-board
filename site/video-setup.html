Add live video to your board

Paste an HLS stream link or a UniFi camera share link, preview it here, and get a short code to type on your board.

Two kinds of links work: an HLS stream (https, usually ending in .m3u8) which the board plays muted, or a UniFi Protect Share Livestream link (monitor.ui.com/...) which embeds UniFi's own player. Share links play in regular browsers but not on Board/Desk hardware (its engine can't complete UniFi's stream handshake) - for boards, restream cameras as HLS instead (e.g. a go2rtc gateway). Anyone with a share link can view that camera; revoke it in Protect to cut access. Use only streams you have the rights to show.
